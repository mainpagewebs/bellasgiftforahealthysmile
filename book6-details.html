Daisy Welcomes Snuggles To A Healthy Heart

The story of a baby penguin named Snuggles who is getting a heart transplant. Daisy takes us on a journey of showing Snuggles a heart gift procedure with Dr. Ollie the heart surgeon and then through the adventure of post procedure care with Dr. Lamb the dentist on how to brush your teeth. This book is for young children to be calm under heart procedures and learn to care after the procedure has concluded! I hope you enjoy this book which teaches your child of the heart procedure and how to brush their teeth!
