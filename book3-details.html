Daisy Teaches Tinsel How To Brush His Teeth

In The Library Of Congress

Description: The story of a baby cow named Daisy, who met a new friend, named Tinsel the Elf. Daisy takes us on a journey of teaching Tinsel how to brush his teeth. This book is for young children to learn how fun it is to brush their teeth! I hope you enjoy this book which teaches your kids about dental hygiene for a brighter and healthier smile! Daisy teaches Tinsel how to brush his teeth for the first time with the help of Dr. Lamb. Daisy takes the reader through a creative and innovative process of teaching about the importance of brushing your teeth.
