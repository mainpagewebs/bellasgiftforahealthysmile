La aventura boliviana de Daisy y Lulu

Description: The story of a cute bird named Lulu, Daisy's friend, who lives in Bolivia. Lulu embarks on a journey guided by Daisy, the baby cow, where she learns which foods are good and bad to maintain healthy teeth. Daisy and Lulu hope you'll learn the innovative and adventurous ways to brush your teeth and which foods you should and shouldn't eat. I hope you enjoy this book which teaches your kids about dental hygiene for a brighter and healthier smile!
