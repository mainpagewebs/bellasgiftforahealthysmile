Daisy's Emergency Dental Check Up

Description: How dentistry teaches children to take care of dental hygiene through the story of a baby cow named Daisy who has chipped her tooth and the magical dentist, Dr. lamb, will help heal her tooth with the help of all children! This book is for young children to read and learn to not be afraid and scared of dentists. Along with learning more about dental hygiene and various procedures dentists perform. The main character Daisy has chipped her tooth while playing around and needs to have an emergency visit to the dentist. Dr. Lamb takes the reader through the innovative and fun way of the process involved with teeth fillings and cleanings for a healthier and brighter smile.
