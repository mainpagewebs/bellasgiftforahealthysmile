About Me

Hello, I am Bella. I am an aspiring high school student looking to advance and further my experience in the dental field. I am writing children's books to teach children worldwide the importance of brushing their teeth along with various sectors of dentistry. My goal is to bring awareness to communities that aren't influenced or don't have the resources to learn about dental care and hygiene. In ensuring I can make a difference in my society I have partnered with Texas Children's Missions to lower income countries worldwide to spread the message of dental care as the doctors and surgeons are performing open heart surgery. Through giving my children's dental books and dental supplies to the children after surgery they learn to take care of themselves to prevent further infections and harm to their heart health. My mission is to achieve a worldwide knowledge of dental care processes and to have the resources to learn more about it!

I hope you and your family can join me in my mission and help inform and save the lives of children across the globe!
